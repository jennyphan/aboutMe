About Me
Hello and welcome to my page. My name is Jenny Phan and during the day I am a full time lead application developer and my passion is dancing. I guess you came to this page because you wanted to know a little bit about me. I'm not that good at talking about myself but I will give it a shot.


Who am I

My love for dancing started when I was just a small child. I always knew I wanted to dance and I would come home from school every day, turn on the music and dance my heart away. I never really had the opportunity as a child to take dance classes as I came from an under privileged family.

In addition to dancing, as a small child I also had a love for computers. I wrote my first computer program using the Commodore 64. I also took many programming classes in high school and it seemed to come fairly easy for me. Throughout my classes in high school I ended up helping many of the other students and was recommended by my teacher to take the advanced classes.

When I went off to attend the University, like most others, I was not sure what I wanted to do. I knew I had a love for dancing and had a natural rhythm to music but I had no experience. Since programming seemed fairly easy to me, paid well and there was a demand for it, I decided to study Computer Science. I decided that once I graduated with my degree, that I would go crazy and take all the dance classes that I could and that is exactly what I did.


Besides dancing, I also enjoy reading, working out and doing yoga. I'm not that great at hand stands and inversions but one day I would like to get really good at them.

As far as my personality traits I would say I am a dedicated person who does not give up easily and will put in the time necessary to accomplish my goals/tasks. I am also very determined and not afraid of hard work

You might be wondering why I chose the name of my site to be k A s i a n B e a u t i I originally chose this domain name because I have an interest in Korean beauty products. I traveled to Korea a few years ago and was intrigued by the focus that the Koreans had on their skin. So I invested a lot of time into studying their products and had decided that at one time I wanted to start selling Korean skin care products online. Thus I came up with the name kasian for Korean American Asian, since I am half Korean and American and added the word beauti at the end.

I started working at a very young age. My mother was really good at finding my brothers and I work. I did pretty much did everything I could to make money. I went Christmas caroling, delivered newspapers and was a babysitter. If there was one thing I learned from my mother, it was the value of hard work. I believe you can be good at anything if you work hard at it. Coming from an under privileged family, I felt I never really fit in , but because of it I never felt I had to follow along with any crowd and I learned to be myself. I never really let other peoples opinions influence me unless it is really someone I admire. I feel that there is no such thing as failure and for every failure there is an opportunity to learn, you will always get something out of trying. I also feel the road to success is the same no matter what you do, it is no different whether you are trying to become the best dancer or design the best application. The same principals apply.
